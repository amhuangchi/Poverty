    Poverty.

    What is Poverty?

    Poverty is the state of being extremely poor, which means that the people suffering from poverty don't have enough money to meet the basics like food, clothing , and shelter. However, poverty is much more than just not having enough money.

    Facts about Poverty

    According to the United Nations, about 21,0000 people die every day of hunger or hunger-related causes. Unfortunately, it is the children that are usually the victims.

    Poverty did not just appear out of nowhere, we the people created it. Poverty can be fixed by us, humans, but most of us chooses not to do anything about it.
    Poverty is one of the worst form of punishment, it is like a punishment for a crime that you didn't commit.
