    Stop Poverty

    Poverty is passed down from generation to generation, innocent people are suffering from poverty all because we are too greedy to share. Most of us decide to turn a blind eye to the fact that millions of people die because of poverty.

    Millions of lives can be saved if everyone donated at least a dollar. Just one simple dollar and millions of lives can be saved. Don't want to sacrifice your dollar for someone you don't know? Well, you can donate your old clothes, shoes, toys, food, etc. Anything is useful! Don't be a greedy person and help the needed.>

    Poverty did not just appear out of nowhere, we the people created it. Poverty can be fixed by us, humans, but most of us chooses not to do anything about it.
    Poverty is one of the worst form of punishment, it is like a punishment for a crime that you didn't commit.
